You can register a Mule app in Anypoint MQ. When registered, Anypoint MQ lists
the client ID and client secret for the app, which you need to configure the Anypoint MQ Connector in Anypoint Studio.

An app does not have to exist for you to register it.

To register an app:

. Log in to Anypoint Platform.
. Click *MQ* in the left navigation bar or the main Anypoint Platform screen.
. Click *Destinations* in the left navigation pane.
. Click *Client Apps* from the left navigation pane:
. Click the blue plus (+) icon:
+
.The arrow shows the blue plus icon at the top of the *Client Apps* page.
image::mq-client-app-add.png[Blue plus icon used for registering a client app]
+
. Specify the name of the client app.
. Click *Save Changes*.
. Click the client app entry to view the client ID and client secret value in the details pane.
+
The client secret value is obfuscated unless you click *Show*.
+
.The screen shows (*1*) the *Client App ID* field, (*2*) *Show* button, and (*3*) *Copy* button.  
image::mq-client-apps-detail.png[Client App ID field, Show and Copy buttons]
+
. Click the *Copy* button for the client ID or client secret to copy the value to your clipboard.
+
Use the client ID and client secret values when configuring the connection types for Anypoint MQ Connector in a Mule app in Anypoint Studio.
